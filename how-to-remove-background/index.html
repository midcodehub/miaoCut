Frequently Asked Questions

What's the easiest way to remove a background?

An AI background remover that runs in your browser is the easiest — you upload an image and download a cutout in about a second, with no tools to learn. MiaoCut does this free, with no signup or watermark. Built-in tools in PowerPoint, GIMP, or Photoshop work too but take more steps.

How do I remove a background for free?

Free options include MiaoCut (browser, one click), GIMP (free desktop software with manual selection tools), and the iPhone Photos app (long-press a subject). Paid tools like Photoshop and a Microsoft Office license also include background removal.

How do I get a transparent background?

Remove the background and export as PNG (or WebP) — these formats support an alpha channel, so the erased area stays truly transparent. JPG cannot store transparency. MiaoCut exports transparent PNG by default.

Which method handles hair and fur best?

Manual tools struggle with soft edges. An AI matting model handles them best — MiaoCut's Fine mode uses alpha matting to keep individual hair strands and fur instead of carving them off.
