Frequently Asked Questions

What ID photo sizes does MiaoCut support?

China 1-inch (295×413), 2-inch (413×579), small/large 1-inch and 2-inch variants, China passport (390×567), US passport (600×600), Schengen visa (600×600), resume photo (480×640), social profile (512×512), plus any custom pixel size.

What background colors are supported?

White, blue, red, gray, light blue, dark blue, pink, black, and any custom HEX color.

Can I print multiple ID photos on one sheet?

Yes. Choose 5-inch, 6-inch, 7-inch, or A4 paper to generate a printable layout sheet with multiple copies arranged for cutting.

Will my photo meet official document requirements?

MiaoCut produces correctly sized photos with neutral backgrounds, but always check your specific document or visa office’s latest requirements (head size, expression, glasses rules, file format) before submitting.

Does MiaoCut detect my face automatically?

Yes. MediaPipe Face Mesh detects your eye line and head position, then the photo is auto-cropped and aligned to fit the chosen size preset. You can fine-tune subject size and top margin with the sliders.

Is my photo private?

Photos are processed in-memory on the server and discarded immediately after the result is returned. We don’t store your image, don’t use it for AI training, and there is no signup required.

Can I set a max file size in KB for online forms?

Yes. The "Target KB" input lets you cap the output file size — useful for visa or job application forms with strict file size limits.

How to Make a Passport or ID Photo

📤

1. Upload your portrait

Upload a front-facing portrait in JPG, PNG, or WebP. Even, neutral lighting works best.

⚙️

2. Pick size and background

Choose a country preset (or enter custom dimensions), pick a background color, and adjust subject size or top margin if needed.

🖼️

3. Generate, fit KB, download

MiaoCut auto-detects your face and produces a standard photo, an HD transparent version, and a printable layout. Set Target KB if your form requires a specific file size.

ID Photo Specs by Country & Document

Common document sizes and background rules. Always check your specific consulate, embassy, or office's latest requirements before submitting — rules change.

China 1-inch (一寸)

295 × 413 px · White / blue / red background · For ID cards, school documents, simple application forms.

China 2-inch (二寸)

413 × 579 px · White / blue / red background · For resumes, diplomas, employment forms, medical reports.

China Passport (中国护照)

390 × 567 px · White background, neutral expression, no glasses recommended · For passport, visa applications submitted in China.

US Passport

600 × 600 px (2 × 2 inch) · Plain white or off-white background · No glasses (since 2016), neutral expression, full face visible.

Schengen Visa (EU)

413 × 531 px (35 × 45 mm) · Light gray or off-white background · 70-80% face coverage, neutral expression.

Japan Visa

600 × 600 px (45 × 45 mm) · White or off-white background · Front-facing, no head covering, taken within 6 months.
