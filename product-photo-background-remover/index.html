Tips for the Cleanest Product Cutouts

  • Shoot with even, diffuse lighting — harsh shadows confuse the edge detector.
  • Keep the camera roughly perpendicular to the product to avoid distortion the AI may misread as a separate object.
  • Use the 2000px or 2048px square canvas when you need a clean e-commerce product image with consistent margins.
  • For jewelry, glass, plastic bottles, or anything reflective, use Fine (hair / fur) mode. Alpha matting handles soft edges much better.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create Amazon-ready white background product photos?

Yes. After the cutout is generated, choose the white background option and export a square product image. Always check the latest marketplace image rules before publishing.

Can I export square product images for Shopify and Etsy?

Yes. The Product page includes 1080px, 2000px, and 2048px square presets, plus subject padding controls for consistent storefront thumbnails.

Can I add a shadow under the product?

Yes. Use the shadow control to export either a clean no-shadow cutout, a soft grounded shadow, or a stronger floating product shadow.

Does it handle reflective items, jewelry, glass, or plastic?

Yes. Switch the quality toggle to "Fine (hair / fur)" mode for products with soft, reflective, or semi-transparent edges. It uses alpha matting for cleaner results on tricky materials.

Can I process product photos in bulk?

This first version processes one image at a time. It is free for normal individual use, with fair-use limits to keep the service stable.
