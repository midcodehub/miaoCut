Tips for the Cleanest Product Cutouts

  • Shoot with even, diffuse lighting — harsh shadows confuse the edge detector.
  • Keep the camera roughly perpendicular to the product to avoid distortion the AI may misread as a separate object.
  • Crop tight before uploading. Smaller frames give the model less background clutter to disambiguate.
  • For jewelry, glass, plastic bottles, or anything reflective, use the Fine (hair / fur) mode — alpha matting handles the soft edges much better.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this background remover for Amazon listings?

Yes. MiaoCut outputs a transparent PNG. To meet Amazon's pure white background requirement, place the cutout on a white canvas in any image editor or use a free online tool like Photopea.

Is the output high enough resolution for Shopify and Etsy?

Yes. MiaoCut preserves the original image resolution up to about 4 megapixels (2048px on the long edge), which exceeds Shopify (2048px recommended) and Etsy (2000px) requirements.

Does it handle reflective items, jewelry, glass, or plastic?

Yes. Switch the quality toggle to "Fine (hair / fur)" mode for products with soft, reflective, or semi-transparent edges. It uses alpha matting for cleaner results on tricky materials.

Can I process product photos in bulk?

MiaoCut currently processes one image at a time, but usage is unlimited and free. There are no daily or monthly caps for casual sellers.

Does the result include a watermark?

No. The output is a clean transparent PNG without any watermark, fully usable for commercial product listings.
