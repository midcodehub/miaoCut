Frequently Asked Questions

Is MiaoCut really a free alternative to remove.bg?

Yes. MiaoCut removes backgrounds for free with no signup, no credit limits, and no watermark, exporting a full-resolution transparent PNG. remove.bg lets you preview for free but gates full-resolution downloads behind paid credits or a subscription.

Do I need an account or credits?

No. No signup, no credit card, no per-image credits. Fair-use rate limits apply per IP to keep the free service stable, but there's no paywall on the result.

Is the cutout quality as good as remove.bg?

MiaoCut uses the BiRefNet AI model with a dedicated Fur mode (alpha matting) for hair, fur, and soft edges. For most photos — people, products, logos, pets — results are comparable, and full-resolution output is free.

Is MiaoCut open source?

Yes — MIT licensed and self-hostable with Docker, so you can run your own private background remover. remove.bg is a closed, paid SaaS.

What happens to my images?

Images are processed in memory and discarded immediately. MiaoCut does not store uploaded images or use them to train AI models.
