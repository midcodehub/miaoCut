Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

MiaoCut (miaocut.app) is a free, no-signup online image-processing service. We designed it to collect as little data as possible. This page explains what happens to your images and what limited data we process.

Your images

  • Processed in memory only. Images you upload are held in server memory just long enough to produce a result, then discarded.
  • Not stored. We do not save uploaded images or results to disk or any database.
  • Never used for training. Your images are never used to train AI models.
  • No human review. No one at MiaoCut looks at your images.

Information we do process

  • IP address (transient). Used only to enforce fair-use rate limits and prevent abuse. It is not used to build a profile of you.
  • Anonymous usage analytics. We use a privacy-friendly analytics tool (Umami) that does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. It records aggregate events like page views and which tool was used.
  • Optional feedback. If you choose to submit feedback, we store the message you send so we can improve the service.

Cookies

MiaoCut does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Your browser may store small preferences locally (such as your chosen language or quality mode) using local storage; this stays on your device.

Service providers

The website is served via Cloudflare, and background processing runs on Hugging Face infrastructure. These providers process requests on our behalf to deliver the service. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

Children

MiaoCut is a general-purpose image tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us through the feedback option on the site or at our GitHub.
